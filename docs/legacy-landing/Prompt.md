PROJECT CONTEXT: GÓC NHÀ MÌNH

Brand:
- Name: Góc Nhà Mình
- Category: cafe / tea / snacks
- Brand feeling: warm, calm, cozy, artisan, friendly, lifestyle
- Audience: students, office workers, people who enjoy chill spaces, working/studying in cafes, light social hangouts
- Main commercial goal: encourage store visits, menu viewing, and map/direction clicks
- Primary CTA: Xem menu / Chỉ đường
- Secondary CTA: Nhắn Zalo / Ghé quán ngay

Visual direction:
- warm coffee brown
- caramel
- cream beige
- soft ivory
- minimal but emotionally rich
- boutique cafe / editorial lifestyle feeling
- not SaaS, not startup, not corporate

Important constraints:
- no favicon
- avoid generic decorative icons
- animation only when scrolled into view
- animation must work on desktop, Android mobile, and iPhone Safari
- use subtle motion only
- no heavy parallax
- no generic hero
- no cluttered layout
- no weak CTA hierarchy
- no repetitive product-grid feel
- no visually empty sections that feel unfinished

The previous version had these problems and they must be actively corrected:
- hero too busy, too many competing layers
- navbar too weak
- intro section not structurally strong
- highlight section too dry and empty
- menu section looked like a demo product grid
- gallery lacked visual rhythm
- promo section was too weak
- testimonial section lacked trust and weight
- contact section felt unbalanced
- footer felt heavy

You must redesign with stronger hierarchy, cleaner composition, better spacing, better content density, and more premium art direction.
